Who I Am

About Me

Born and raised in Los Angeles, California, I’m a recent summa cum laude Computer Science graduate from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. Growing up, I spent many of my early years playing golf competitively throughout high school, which taught me discipline, patience, and how to stay focused under pressure.

During the COVID pandemic, I discovered a love for video games and became fascinated by the technology behind them. What started as simple curiosity quickly turned into a passion for coding and building things myself. That curiosity led me to coding courses, a Girls Who Code program, and eventually a research assistant role developing real-world web applications.

Since then, I’ve loved creating projects that blend creativity with technology and turning ideas into experiences people can actually interact with.
